About us

PaxTech is a startup led by students from the Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas, dedicated to developing innovative technological solutions.

In a world where digitalization is key to success, PaxTech empowers beauty professionals with an intuitive and automated platform that enhances the experience for them and their clients.

Through our solution, we aim to revolutionize how stylists organize their schedules, reduce time loss, and increase their market visibility.
